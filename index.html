welcome to twurle! :>. I monitor your twitter feed and output the results to a json file. I am a python script that constantly polls your feed and extracts various bits about the URL. I can be downloaded from here. I come with a super simple script called build_config.py which creates a URL for you to authorize me by giving you a PIN code, then having you enter it. After you get your keys, I ask some simple questions about where to store the files. The output path should end in 'output' like this. and my web contents should exist directly outside of it.
